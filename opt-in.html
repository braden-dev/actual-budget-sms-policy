SMS Opt-In

Braden DeVargas Actual Budget SMS Assistant is a private household budgeting SMS program operated by Braden DeVargas.

How to Opt In

Text START to +1 (757) 697-0687.

By texting START, you consent to receive recurring automated budget-related text messages from Braden DeVargas Actual Budget SMS Assistant. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchasing or using any other service. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to cancel.

Messages You May Receive

  • Responses to budget questions you send by SMS.
  • Transaction review and uncategorized transaction reminders.
  • Weekly spending or transaction summaries.
  • Monthly budget status updates.

Confirmation Message

Braden DeVargas Actual Budget SMS Assistant: You are opted in to recurring budget texts. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel.

Help and Opt-Out

  • Reply HELP for help.
  • Reply STOP to cancel.
  • Reply START to opt back in after opting out.
