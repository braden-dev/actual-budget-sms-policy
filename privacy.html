Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Braden DeVargas Actual Budget SMS Assistant is a private SMS service operated by Braden DeVargas for approved household budgeting messages.

Information We Collect

We collect mobile phone numbers, inbound SMS message contents, outbound SMS message contents, and message delivery metadata needed to provide the SMS service.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to send and receive budget-related SMS messages, answer budget questions, send scheduled budget summaries, and operate the private SMS assistant.

Mobile Number and Opt-In Data

Mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, and SMS consent records are not sold, rented, shared, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties except service providers required to operate the SMS program.

Service Providers

We may use service providers, such as SMS delivery and hosting providers, only as needed to operate the SMS assistant. These providers are not permitted to use mobile phone numbers or SMS consent data for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

SMS Program Disclosures

Message frequency varies based on user questions and configured weekly or monthly budget reports. Message and data rates may apply. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing or using another service.

Message Controls

  • Reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages.
  • Reply START to opt back in after opting out.
  • Reply HELP for help information.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the SMS program, reply HELP to +1 (757) 697-0687 to contact the operator.
