Braden DeVargas Actual Budget SMS Assistant is a private SMS service operated by Braden DeVargas for approved household budgeting messages.

Program Description

Approved household members may receive SMS replies to budget questions, transaction review reminders, scheduled budget summaries, and related private budgeting notifications.

Consent

End users opt in only after reviewing the public SMS Opt-In Instructions and texting START to +1 (757) 697-0687. Phone numbers are not enrolled solely through verbal permission or configuration by the operator. Consent applies only to this private budgeting SMS program and is not transferable.

Message Frequency and Cost

Message frequency varies based on user questions and configured weekly or monthly budget reports. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out and Help

  • Reply STOP to cancel and opt out.
  • Reply START to opt back in after opting out.
  • Reply HELP for help or contact the operator at +1 (757) 697-0687.

Delivery

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery is subject to carrier network availability and is not guaranteed.

Availability

This service is private and available only to mobile numbers explicitly approved by the operator.
